Spinach Lasagna

Vegetarian spinach lasagna

This lasagna is tasty while keeping the calories low.

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 400g spinach
  • 1/2 onion
  • 200g diced tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • oil
  • 150g light cream cheese
  • 70g Parmesan cheese
  • lasagna noodles as needed
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • some fat for the baking dish
  • paprica powder
  • nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon basil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. cube the onion and heat it in some oil
  2. mix the spinach and the cream cheese in
  3. let everything heat up
  4. add the garlic and tomatoes
  5. stir and season it with some nutmeg, paprica powder, basil, salt and pepper
  6. cook it for 5 minutes
  7. grease the baking dish
  8. layer the lasagna noodles with the mixture
  9. add some Parmesan cheese as needed
  10. bake it for 45 minutes at 175 degree celcius
